Tandoori cooking is a hallmark of Indian cuisine, and Top of India does it perfectly. Their tandoori menu includes skewered meats such as chicken, lamb, and fish marinated in a blend of spices and cooked to perfection in a traditional clay oven. 3. Biryani

Try one of the restaurant’s Biryani dishes for a hearty and satisfying meal. This fragrant rice dish, cooked with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and your choice of meat or vegetables, is the perfect comfort food. Whether you go for chicken, lamb, or vegetarian, the Biryani at Top of India is an experience.

4. Vegetarian Dishes

Vegetarian Indian food is diverse, flavorful, and satisfying, and Top of India offers an array of vegetarian dishes to suit every taste. From Aloo Gobi (spiced potatoes and cauliflower) to Baingan Bharta (smoked eggplant), the vegetarian options are as flavorful and well-crafted as their meat counterparts.

5. Naan and Roti

No Indian meal is complete without a side of fresh naan or roti. Top of India offers various traditional breads, including garlic naan, butter naan, and roti. They make the perfect accompaniment to any curry, helping to soak up the rich sauces and enhancing the overall experience.

Don’t forget to finish your meal with a sweet treat! Top of India serves popular Indian desserts like Gulab Jamun (sweet dough balls soaked in syrup) and Ras Malai (rich cream and cardamom-flavored milk with soft cheese).
